I am currently a Research Fellow in the School of Computing in National University of Singapore, advised by Prof. Bryan Hooi. I am also an associate member at SEA AI Lab. Previously, I worked at Nanyang Technological University and obtained my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Singapore University of Technology and Design and B.Eng. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Zhejiang University. My research interests focus on large language model agents and efficient large language models. Feel free to contact me if you are interested in any discussions!
